Comedian

My debut stand up gig was in Dunedin, May 2017, since then I have performed across the country in every NZ city, alongside Jeremy Elwood, Brendhan Lovegrove, Tim Batt and more. I have also performed in numerous festivals including: Dunedin Fringe Festival(s), The Christchurch Comedy Carnival, Fringe in the Stings', Salty Buskers Festival, and the Palmy Fringe Festival.
