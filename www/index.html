Barking Mad!

This thursday, Demelza Ray will take us down the rabbit hole. So, don your fanciest tea party attire, pack your mysterious "eat me" cakes, and join us for an evening in wonderland 👱‍♀️🍄🎩🐰

Headliner: Tony Lyall, one of New Zealand's most exciting new comedians. With a confident style and an endearing nature, Tony takes the audience on a trip, even if he's not always sure where they will end up. Tony is a reporter and writer for The Project, has a segment on The Rock and writes for 7 Days. He's also a bloody great guy, three cheers for Tony!

"Sharp, engaging and consistently funny." - Appetite for the Arts

Also featuring a bunch of magical members from The Mad Hatter's table
