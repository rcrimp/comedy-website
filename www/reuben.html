Comedian

My first open mic performance was on May 2017 here in Dunedin and since then I have gigged in every NZ city. I have sharaed the stage with some of New Zealand's best comedians Jeremy Elwood, Brendhan Lovegrove, Britney Cardwell... I have performed at numerous festivals: Dunedin Fringe Festival(s), The Christchurch Comedy Carnival, Fringe in the Stings', Salty Buskers Festival, and the Palmy Fringe Festival.

Comedy Producer

I have been producing shows as Dunedin Comedy since early 2018. As a necessity I have taken a variety of roles in the production pipeline including: booking talent, scouting for talent, marketing, graphic design, audio, lighting, filming, photography, stage management. Alongside Dunedin Comedy productions I also been hired to work for the Dunedin Fringe Festival and the NZ Young Writers Festival, .
