Ngā tīpuna o Ōtepoti

An incomplete and unfinished history of Dunedin Comedy

Dunedin has produced many great comedians over the years. The University of Otago has long been the backbone of Dunedin Comedy. The Capping Show has provided stage opportunities. The Dunedin Fringe Festival has long been a supporter of comedy as a performance art.
